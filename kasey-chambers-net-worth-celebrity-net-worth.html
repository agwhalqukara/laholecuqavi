What is Kasey Chambers' Net Worth?

Kasey Chambers is an Australian county singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $9 million. Kasey Chambers was born in Mount Gambier, South Australia, Australia in June 1976. Her father is singer and musician Bill Chambers and her brother is musician and producer Nash Chambers. Kasey Chambers released her debut studio album The Captain in 1999.

Her second album Barricades & Brickwalls was released in 2001 and reached #1 in Australia and on the US Heatseekers chart and was certified 7x Platinum in Australia. Chambers' next three albums Wayward Angel in 2004, Carnival in 2006, and Rattlin' Bones (with Shane Nicholson) in 2008 all reached #1 in Australia. She went on to release six more albums including Dragonfly in 2017. Her single "Not Pretty Enough" reached #1 in Australia where it was certified 2x Platinum.
