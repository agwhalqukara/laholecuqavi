Solid perfumes: The chic portable scents we're loving

We picked our favourite solid perfumes for fresh fragrance on the go

    Solid perfumes are the perfect functional accessory for the Christmas party season. A stylish and fun way to carry your favourite scent, they're often enclosed in gorgeous wearable jewellery too.

    Plus, they're super convenient and portable, so they're ideal to replace liquid scents if you're travelling.

    Here's the best solid perfumes we think deserve a spot in your handbag...

    Burberry My Burberry gold solid perfume, £30, available at Selfridges

    My Burberry fragrance is inspired by the label's iconic trench coat. Bursting with notes of Sweet Pea, Freesia Heart and Patchouli, this solid perfume captures the essence of an English garden after rainfall and is designed to melt into your skin.
