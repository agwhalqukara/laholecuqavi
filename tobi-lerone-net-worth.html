About

Best known as either Tobjizzle or TBJZL, he is a FIFA gamer who publishes videos to YouTube and also streams gameplay using the broadcasting Service Twitch. His TBJZL YouTube channel has earned over 2.9 million subscribers.

Before Fame

The first couple of videos he posted to his main channel TBJZL featured the video games Call of Duty, Fable II, and Minecraft. It wasn't long until FIFA became his primary game of choice. 

Trivia

He has a second channel called EDITinGAMING where he plays games against a group of friends that would become known as the Ultimate Sidemen. 

Family Life

His real name is Tobi Brown and he was born at Homerton University Hospital in London, England. His brother is YouTuber FIFAManny. He often posts photos of his dog on his Instagram.

Associated With

He is joined in the Ultimate Sidemen by Miniminter, Zerkaa, KSI, Behzinga, and Vikkstar.
