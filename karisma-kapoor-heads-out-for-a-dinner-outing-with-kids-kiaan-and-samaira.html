Karisma Kapoor heads out for a dinner outing with kids Kiaan and Samaira

It’s a known fact that Karisma Kapoor is a doting mamma to her kids Kiaan Raj Kapoor and daughter Samaira Kapoor. The stunner recently returned from a London vacation where she was seen spending time with her children, as well as sister Kareena Kapoor Khan and mom Babita Kapoor.


Karisma had the perfect Saturday night out with Kiaan and Samaira. The trio was snapped posing for the paparazzi present outside a popular eatery in the city. Looking absolutely gorgeous, Karisma kept it simple and stylish in a classic white tee which she paired with jeans.
