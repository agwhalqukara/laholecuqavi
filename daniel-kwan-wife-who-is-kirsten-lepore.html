Daniel Kwan Wife: Who is Kirsten Lepore?

The Daniels are Daniel Kwan and his partner, Daniel Scheinert. They are American cinema directors, producers, and screenwriters.

They began their career as music video makers, including the popular DJ Snake and Lil Jon music video for the tune “Turn Down for What” (2013).

They have since delved into film, writing and directing the surreal black comedy-drama Swiss Army Man (2016) and the science-fiction picture Everything Everywhere All at Once (2022).

The latter became A24’s highest-grossing film of all time, and the Daniels were nominated for Academy Awards for Best Picture, Best Director, and Best Original Screenplay.

Daniel Kwan’s Wife: Who is Kirsten Lepore?

Kirsten Lepore, Daniel Kwan’s wife, is an animator and fellow filmmaker. Sweet Dreams, Bottle, and Move Mountain are among Kirsten’s stop-motion short films.

According to her Twitter bio, Lepore also works as a director. Her animated short film Bottle garnered her international acclaim.

At the same time, Creativity Online named her one of the year’s most influential creatives.
